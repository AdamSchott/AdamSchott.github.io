Welcome to Meals on Wheels!

Serving nutritious, hot meals for 50 years to those in need!




Our Vision



" The vision of the Meals on Wheels is to make a difference in the lives of all who are unable to cook for themselves by providing hot and nutritious meals delivered by volunteers. Hot meals are delivered Monday through Friday to clients who cannot prepare them for themselves. "





Strategy #1
Meals on Wheels relies on 225 volunteers who deliver over 56,000 meals a year to our clients. The impact they are making in the lives of those they serve is immeasurable.
